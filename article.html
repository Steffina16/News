Local Girl Accidentally Becomes Someone's Favorite Person Forever

By Stephen • Love Correspondent

It began like any ordinary day. Nothing unusual. Nothing dramatic. Just another moment that quietly changed everything.

According to eyewitness reports (which, to be fair, are mostly just me), there was one smile... one conversation... and somehow everything after that felt different.

Days turned into weeks. Weeks turned into months. And somewhere along the way, you stopped being just another person. You became the first notification I looked for. The first person I wanted to tell good news to. The last person I wanted to say goodnight to.

Experts remain confused as to how one person can be this beautiful, this caring, and somehow still make my heart race after all this time. Further investigation is ongoing.

Authorities have officially concluded that I don't ever want this story to end.

Because every new day with you... becomes another headline worth writing. ❤️
